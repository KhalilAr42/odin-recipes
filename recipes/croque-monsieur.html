Croque monsieur

Description

Croque Monsieur is a delicious French ham and cheese sandwich made with gruyere, parmesan, ham and a simple béchamel sauce, toasted in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Bechamel sauce
  • Ham – Preferably smoked (who doesn’t love smoked ham?), but any ham is good here.
  • Gruyere and Swiss cheese – The main cheeses! Gruyere is an Alpine cheese from Switzerland. It has a pleasantly sweet and nutty flavour,
  • Parmesan – Alpine cheeses are relatively mild, so parmesan boosts the savoury oomph and saltiness to just where we want it
  • Butter – For pan-searing our Croque Monsieur in buttery goodness before it’s finished in the oven!

Instructions

  1. Spread béchamel on bread. Lay all of your bread slices on the counter and spread a thin layer of béchamel sauce on each slice.
  2. Add ham and cheese. Add one layer of ham and a sprinkle of cheese to half of the bread slices.
  3. Top with bread. Top the ham and cheese with another slice of bread, making sure to leave the béchamel facing up.
  4. Finishing touches. Sprinkle the top layer of bread with more parmesan and gruyere.
  5. Bake. until bread is toasted and cheese is melted and bubbly.
